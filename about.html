How To Use

This tool is meant to be a personal tracker for your academic progress. To use it to its full advantage, add your classes and assignments to the site on the overview page. Then input your score and it will calculate your grade for you.

  • Go to Canvas find your current classes and see which assignments are available.
  • Open the Overview page on this dashboard and use the forms to add those classes.
  • Update your assignment scores as you get them to see a clear, simple view of your GPA.
